About Sanstha


Shreeyash Technical Campus is spread over 26.78 acres land offers comprehensive education by developing the intellectual strength of the students and guiding them towards technical and management advancement and inspires creativity.


STC fosters excellence and innovation in teaching and learning so as to realize our vision as premier Engineering Institution. STC strives to set high standards through Quality Teaching, Research, Knowledge Management through Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, Time-Bound goals and continues Improvement Methodology. Our educational programs have been at the cutting edge since their inception. The dedicated faculty and the supporting staff are the pillars of strength, combined with vibrant and talented students community with human values certainly make us as the institute of National importance.

The modern computing facilities made available to the students are also sought, both by Industry and the nation's most competitive graduate schools for conducting competitive examination.

SIMSR offers high end teaching learning environment for students. It also offers experiential learning by providing well-equipped laboratories.

We, at STC, Shreeyash Technical Campus, have embarked upon a mission and are committed to provide wholesome education in management sciences to enable aspiring students to become professionally competent and industry ready. The infrastructure, dedicated & highly qualified faculties, state of the art Library, IT infrastructure etc are in place to give shape & finesse to our well structured programs.


These go a long way in shaping & honing attitudes & personality which have a direct bearing on our students excelling professionally.
